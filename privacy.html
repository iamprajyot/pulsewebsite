Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pulse. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use the Pulse iOS application.

2. Information Collection and Use

Personal Data

We do not collect any personal data (such as your name, email address, or phone number) through the Pulse app. All of your interval configurations, sounds, and settings are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

Usage Data & Third Parties

The free version of Pulse is supported by advertisements. We use Google AdMob to provide these advertisements. Since we do not track you across other companies' apps and websites (App Tracking Transparency is not used), AdMob is configured to serve non-personalized ads only. This means the ads are contextual and are not based on your past behavior.

However, Google AdMob may still collect limited, non-personalized information (such as your device type, OS version, and general location) for purposes like frequency capping, aggregated reporting, and combating fraud. You can learn more about how Google uses data when you use their partners' apps by visiting Google's Privacy & Terms site.

3. Pulse Pro (In-App Purchases)

If you choose to upgrade to Pulse Pro, the transaction is processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not process or have access to your payment information. Apple provides us with anonymous receipt data so we can unlock the premium features on your device.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at mail2prajyot@gmail.com or through our Support page.
